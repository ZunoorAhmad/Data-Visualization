Thinger.io is an Open-Source platform created to simplify IoT product development.

Our ready-to-go connexion infrastructure allows to manage devices, store, monitor and analyze data from thousands of IoT sources.

The premise is quite simple, use our cloud server to connect your devices or create your own IoT server with the Thinger.io instance, then develop and share your products with a full customizable graphical interface.
